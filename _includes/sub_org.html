ACM-W

Our mission is to empower and uplift students, with a particular focus on supporting women in technology, by fostering an inlcusive, dynamic, and collaborative environment.


We strive to:

  • Empower women and underrepresented students to thrive in tech though mentorship, leadership roles, and skill-building opportunities.
  • Enhance academic and professional development via workshops, career panels, speaker vents, and resume reviews.
  • Foster social connections and a sense of belonging through mixers, game nights, and team-building activities.
  • Building a vibrant, supportive network of peers, alumni, and industry professionals.
  • Promote confidence and curiosity, encouraging members to explore new areas of computing and push boundaries.
